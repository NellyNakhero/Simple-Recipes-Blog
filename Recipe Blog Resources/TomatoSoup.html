Tomato Soup

Serves 4|Time 26-33 min

Ingredients:

  • 750g ripe tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • thinly sliced 1 clove garlic
  • 1 tablespoon tomato sauce
  • 1 carrot
  • finely chopped 500 ml vegetable or chicken stock 1 teaspoon sugar

Procedure

  1. Make a slit in the tomatoes and microwave them at 750 W for 6-8 minutes, until they are easy to peel.
  2. Peel and dice the tomatoes.
  3. Place the oil, onion and garlic in a shallow microwaveable tray.
  4. Cook uncovered for 3-4 minutes at 750 W.
  5. Add the tomato sauce, the diced tomato and the chopped carrot, cover and cook for a further 4-5 minutes at 750 W.
  6. Add the sugar while stirring. Cover and cook for 8-10 minutes at 750 W. Stir until you are left with a smooth, creamy mixture.
  7. Pour over the vegetable or chicken stock and cook, uncovered, for 5-6 minutes at 750 W

An extra special touch:
Add fresh basil leaves and freshly ground pepper, or scramble some eggs and add them to the soup before serving
