Mushroom and Saffron Risotto

Serves 4|Time 25-28 min

Ingredients:

  • 1 small onion
  • chopped 1 clove garlic crushed
  • 25 g butter
  • 30 g dried mushrooms
  • 1 sachet saffron
  • 350 g rice
  • 100 ml dry white wine
  • 50 g cheese
  • grated 700 ml savoury stock (hot)

Procedure

  1. Soak the mushrooms in warm water, chop into pieces and mix them with the onion and butter in a microwaveable container;
  2. cover and cook for 4 minutes at 750 W, stirring once halfway through.
  3. Add the rice and the wine, stir and leave to evaporate, uncovered, for 3 minutes at 750 W.
  4. Pour in the hot stock and saffron. The stock can be heated in a microwaveable container for 5-6 minutes.
  5. Stir, cover and cook at 750 W for 6-7 minutes. Cover and continue cooking at 750 W for 7-8 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven, add the grated cheese and stir well.
  7. Season to taste and leave to stand for a few minutes before serving.
