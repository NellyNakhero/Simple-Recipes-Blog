Jacket Potatoes

Serves 2| Time 16-22 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 large potatoes, with skin (approximately 200-250 g each)

Procedure

  1. Wash the potatoes thoroughly and prick with a fork.
  2. Place in a microwaveable dish, over and cook for 12-15 minutes at 750 W.
  3. Turn the potatoes halfway through and finish by cooking for a further 4-5 minutes, still at 750 W.

An extra special touch:
Make a slit in the potatoes and fill with butter and salt, or melted cheese.
